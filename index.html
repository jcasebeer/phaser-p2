Controls:
Switch Cat: z
Shoot: x
aim turret: a + d
steer tank: left + right
gas: up
break: down

Tank Catz

  • High Concept:
    Control 4 different cat's simaltaneously to win the war and save your cactuses!
  • Player Experience Goals:
    Players should feel overwhelmed by having to control each aspect of
    the tank manually by switching between cats.
  • Genre: First person shooter / arcade game.
  • Game play: The player must pilot the tank through the eyes of each of its cuddly crew members.
    The player can change viewpoints with the z key, shoot with x, steer their tank with the
    arrow keys, and independently aim with the a and d keys.
    The main goal in the game is surviving the onslaught of enemy tanks using these skills.
    If I had more time I would have added two more cats, one which the player needs to use to sweep for mines, and another which can repair the tank.
    The goal of the control layout was to make the player have to stretch their hands out as uncomfortably as
    possible to control each of the cats.
  • Prototype goal: pseduo 3d graphics
    complicated control scheme
  • Features: infinite, persistent field of cacti
    weird controls
  • Setting/Premise: There are 4 cats in a tank, there are other tanks that want to kill you, and you are living in cactus land.
  • Story: N/A
  • Target Audience: The game is targeted at people who like cats, cactuses, and tanks.
  • Play time: Infinite at the moment. (no end state)
  • Strategies:
    One good strategy is to have a friend steer the tank while you shoot the guns!
  • Prototype Assets: All of the assets are from scratch except for the meow sound effect. I also recycled the shooting and explosion sounds from the previous project.
